The M150 is a MEMS Analyzer used for characterizing microstructural mechanical properties.

Using a simple point-and-shoot laser optical system, the user can quickly obtain resonant peaks of a microstructure through wide bandwidth frequency scanning and real-time Fourier transform (FFT).

Non-contact and zero mass-loading, resulting in no
change in stiffness to the sample during measurement

Technical Specifications

Wavelength of laser: 650 nm

Laser output: 0.23 mW

Measuring range: 20 µm

Focus distance: 3.3 mm

Minimum beam diameter: 3 µm

Focus function: Manual

Displacement resolution: 1.23 nm

Measurement frequency range: 1Hz~4.2MHz

Number of input channels: 2

Sampling fequency: Up to 65MHz

Data provide: Time domain, FFT

Waveform: Sinusoidal wave

A/D resolution: 14 bit

Stage displacement X-Y: 2.5 cm

Stage displacement Z: 1cm

Displacement precision X-Y: 2.5 µm

Displacement precision Z: 2.5 µm

Output frequency: Up to 5 MHz

Field of view: 300 µm x 300 µm; 1 cm x 1 cm

Image resolution: 2 µm
