RANGE ROVER EVOQUE

Special Edition with Victoria Beckham

The Range Rover Evoque Special Edition brings together high fashion and design innovation thanks to the collaboration of the Land Rover Design team and Victoria Beckham.

As the winner of the 2011 Designer Brand of the Year, Victoria Beckham is the perfect partner for the Range Rover Evoque, itself the recipient of more than 100 international awards.

Luxury Fashion Film by Nick Knight

" We wanted to create a Special Edition that extends its character in a new direction through understated, restrained colour and detail changes."

Gerry McGovern

DESIGN DIRECTOR, LAND ROVER

" It's the small detail that makes all the difference. I want everyone who gets into this car to feel special and empowered by its quality and craftsmanship. I like that it feels luxurious but also has quite a classic edge to it as well as a contemporary feel."

Victoria Beckham

FASHION DESIGNER
Victoria's View

Essential to the design was the development of a hand-finished matt paint that gives the car a stealth-like appearance.

UNIQUE HAND FINISHED "STORNOWAY GREY"

" Victoria herself inspired the use of rose gold. She was wearing dark clothes and no jewellery apart from a rose gold, man’s watch. I was struck by the juxtaposition of the masculine watch on a very elegant, feminine wrist." GERRY MCGOVERN ON THE EXTERNAL ROSE GOLD ACCENTS

The sport design four-seats are finished in a semi-aniline Vintage Tan leather.

The same leather used to cover the door inserts, arm rests and central storage box lid.

The bold, hand-finished ‘baseball’ stitch detail was chosen by Victoria as a tongue-in-cheek reference to her sporting husband David.

Inside, the application of rose-gold can be found on the centre console rotary switches and Drive Select Rotary Shifter.INTERNAL ROSE GOLD ACCENTS

BESPOKE OWNERS MANUAL

A hand stitched owners manual with rose-gold detail is co-branded Range Rover Evoque Special Edition and signed by Victoria Beckham. It’s your guide to the exclusive features of the car.

FOUR PIECE LUGGAGE SET

Each Range Rover Evoque Special Edition comes with an exclusive four-piece leather luggage in hand stitched black leather. Comprising his and hers two wheeled suitcases and wash bags, they are lined with microsuede with smoked chrome zip to compliment the matt grey exterior finish.

200*

The Range Rover Evoque Special Edition is limited to a maximum of 200 vehicles worldwide. The vehicle is a four-seat coupé, powered by the lightweight, all-aluminium 240PS 2.0-litre petrol engine and features four-wheel drive with Land Rover's award-winning Terrain Response system.
